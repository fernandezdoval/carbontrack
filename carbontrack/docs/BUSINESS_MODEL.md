# CarbonTrack - Business Model & Go-to-Market

## Executive Summary

**Problem:** SMBs need carbon accounting for regulatory compliance and customer expectations, but existing tools are enterprise-focused, complex, and expensive.

**Solution:** CarbonTrack - simple, affordable carbon accounting that works like QuickBooks for emissions.

**Market Size:** 
- 33M SMBs in the US alone
- Growing regulatory pressure (SEC climate disclosure, EU CSRD)
- Customer/investor demand for transparency

**Revenue Model:** SaaS subscription with tiered pricing

## Pricing Strategy

### Freemium Model

**Free Tier:**
- 1 user
- 1 facility
- Manual data entry
- Basic calculations
- 12 months data history
- Community support
- CarbonTrack branding on reports

**Purpose:** Acquire users, demonstrate value, word-of-mouth

**Starter - $29/month** ($290/year)
- 3 users
- 3 facilities
- Automated calculations
- Unlimited history
- CSV export
- Email support
- Remove branding

**Business - $99/month** ($990/year)
- 10 users
- 10 facilities
- API integrations (utility providers)
- Advanced analytics
- Priority support
- White-label reports
- Multi-region support

**Enterprise - Custom**
- Unlimited users/facilities
- Custom integrations
- Dedicated account manager
- SLA guarantees
- On-premise option
- Training & onboarding

### Add-ons
- **Receipt OCR:** $10/month (auto-extract data from receipts)
- **Carbon Offsets Marketplace:** 10% commission
- **Audit Service:** $500-2000/year (third-party verification)

## Risk Mitigation

### Risks

1. **Regulatory Changes:** Requirements could change
   - *Mitigation:* Stay updated, flexible data model

2. **Enterprise Competitors Move Down-market:**
   - *Mitigation:* Speed to market, brand loyalty, better UX

3. **Low Willingness to Pay:** SMBs don't see value
   - *Mitigation:* Free tier proves value, regulatory pressure increases

4. **Data Accuracy Concerns:**
   - *Mitigation:* Use authoritative sources (EPA, IPCC), transparent methodology

### Success Factors

- **Time to Value:** <15 minutes to first calculation
- **Accuracy:** Match or beat consultant calculations
- **Trust:** Transparent sources, auditable
- **Support:** Fast, helpful, educational
